Emotion Recognition from Facial Expressions
  • Collaborated with a team to develop a deep learning model that recognizes human emotions based on facial expressions.
  • Evaluated six unique architectures including Basic CNN, Simple ResNet, GoogleNet variant, UNet, NewNet, and RogleNet.
  • Utilized data augmentation techniques and custom loss functions such as weighted cross-entropy.
  • Enhanced model performance through optimization algorithms including SGD and Adam.
Deep Reinforcement Learning for Multi-Agent Games
  • Implemented reinforcement learning algorithms such as DQN, DDQN, Actor-Critic, and Advantage Actor-Critic in Python.
  • Applied these models to custom-built environments like Tic-Tac-Toe, Rock-Paper-Scissors, and a Switch game.
  • Leveraged experience replay and other advanced techniques to improve model learning, achieving over 92% accuracy.
  • Emphasized reusability and modular design for model experimentation and comparison.
